Quick Answers

Common questions
before reaching out.

Do I need to buy anything to talk to you?

No. Send a message or book a call — it's just a conversation. We'll figure out together what makes sense for your situation.

Can you help us if our alumni base is mostly inactive?

That's actually one of the most common situations we work with. The kit includes outreach templates specifically designed to re-engage dormant alumni.

Does the kit work for virtual programs?

Yes — everything is designed to work virtually or in-person. Many successful programs run entirely online, especially for alumni who've moved away from campus.

We serve multiple chapters. Can we use one kit?

Yes. The kit is designed to be adapted per chapter — swap in the chapter name and details. Reach out and we'll talk through the best setup for your situation.

How long does a Pro Implementation Call take?

60–90 minutes on Zoom. By the end you'll have customized documents, a clear 30-day plan, and answers to your specific questions.

Do you work with K–12 schools directly?

Yes — K–12 schools and districts are a core part of what we do. Send us a message and tell us about your program setup.
