Before You Buy

What people ask us.

What format are the files in?

Google Docs and Google Sheets. You copy them to your own Drive and customize for your institution. No proprietary software. No platform to learn.

Is this a subscription?

No. One payment. You own the documents forever and can reuse them across as many cohorts as you run.

How do the five kits differ?

Same operational framework — intake, matching, communication, accountability, reporting. Different language, examples, and workflows. The Greek Life kit references chapter officers and new member classes. The K-12 kit references counselors, grade levels, and building protocols. Each kit fits the context it was built for.

What happens after I buy?

You receive an email within minutes with a link to your private Google Drive folder. Inside: your kit files organized across 7 labeled subfolders (A through G). Open subfolder G first — it contains the Quick-Start Guide for your program type.

Can I use this for multiple programs?

Yes. If your institution runs mentorship across different contexts — say a university with both a Greek life program and a first-gen student initiative — purchase the kit for each. Email us for multi-kit pricing.
