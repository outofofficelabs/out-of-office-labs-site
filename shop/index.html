FAQ

Common questions.

What format are the kits in?

Google Docs and Sheets. You copy them to your own Drive and customize for your institution. No proprietary platform needed.

Is this a subscription?

No. One-time purchase. You own the documents and can use them for as many cohorts as you want.

Are all five kits the same?

They share the same operational framework — intake, matching, communication, accountability, reporting — but the language, workflows, and examples are tailored to each audience. A Greek chapter kit references chapter officers and pledge classes. A K-12 kit references counselors and grade levels.

What happens after I buy?

You'll be redirected to a page with your download links and receive an email with the same links. Copy the Google Docs to your Drive, customize, and launch.

Can I buy more than one kit?

Yes. If your institution runs mentorship across multiple audiences — say a university with both a Greek life program and a first-gen student program — purchase each kit separately.

Not sure which kit fits?

Every kit uses the same proven system. Pick the one that matches your audience and you're ready to launch.
