What's Inside

Every kit ships with the full operating system.

No half-baked templates. Each component is designed to work together — from first intake to final impact report.

Intake & Application Forms

Pre-built forms for mentors and mentees. Capture goals, availability, preferences, and boundaries upfront.

Matching Logic Framework

Data-informed pairing criteria and scoring rubrics. Match on goals, experience level, availability — not random assignment.

Communication Templates

Welcome sequences, meeting nudges, mid-point check-ins, and wrap-up messages. Pre-written, editable, ready to send.

Accountability Checklists

Week-by-week task lists for mentors, mentees, and coordinators. Everyone knows what's expected and when.

Impact Reporting Templates

Board-ready recap documents. Attendance data, engagement scores, qualitative highlights — formatted for deans, boards, or HQ.

Program Playbook

The coordinator's guide. Timeline, escalation paths, FAQ responses, and a "what to do when things go sideways" runbook.

FAQ

Common questions.

What format are the kits in?

Google Docs and Sheets you can copy, edit, and brand for your institution. No proprietary platform needed.

Is this a subscription?

No. One-time purchase. You own the documents and can use them for as many cohorts as you want.

Which kit is right for my organization?

Single Kit for one pilot, Pro Kit for multi-chapter or 50+ pairs, Campus License for full institutions, District License for multi-building deployments. Not sure? Reach out and we'll help you figure it out.

Can I upgrade later?

Yes. Start with a Single Kit, and if you need to scale, contact us to apply your purchase toward a higher tier.
