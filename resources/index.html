How It Works

in four weeks.

Read the Program Handbook and Timeline. Understand the full program flow so you can explain it confidently to your team or advisory board. Takes 30–45 minutes.

What We've Learned

5 mistakes to avoid
before you launch.

01

Overthinking the launch

Don't wait for perfect. Send outreach this week with what you have. Done beats perfect every time in a first-semester program.

02

Generic mass emails

Personalize at least the first line. Mention a specific memory, game, or connection. The templates give you the structure — you add the personal touch.

03

No follow-up

70% of alumni will ignore the first message. Follow up 3–5 days later. The kit includes follow-up scripts so you're never starting from scratch.

04

Skipping the kickoff event

This is where momentum starts. Make it happen even if only 5 people show up. The energy from that first event carries the program forward.

05

Micromanaging pairs

Trust mentors and mentees to drive their own relationship. Your job is to facilitate and remove friction — not babysit. The kit builds that in.
