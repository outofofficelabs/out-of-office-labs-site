About

Built by an operator, not a consultant.

Out of Office Labs is a product company that builds mentorship operating systems for institutions. No consulting retainers. No platform subscriptions. Just structured systems your team can own and run.

The Founder

25 years of building, leading, and advising.

Philosophy

What we believe.

Mentorship needs structure to work.

Good intentions aren't a system. A program without intake, matching, tracking, and reporting is a program that fizzles by week four.

Institutions deserve products, not projects.

A consulting engagement ends. A platform subscription drains budget. A product you own keeps producing value as long as you run it.

Lean teams should be able to run great programs.

You shouldn't need five FTEs and a six-figure budget to run mentoring that actually moves the needle. Our kits are designed for the one-person office.

Proof matters more than vibes.

Every kit includes impact reporting. Because when your dean or board asks "is this working?" you should have an answer backed by data, not anecdotes.
